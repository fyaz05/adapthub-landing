# How to score 99 percentile in CAT (free-friendly)

**URL:** https://adapthub.in/how-to-score-99-percentile-cat/  
**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Direct answer

99 percentile is mainly a selection-and-accuracy problem under sectional timers—not maximum attempts. Run four systems: prioritise high-weight syllabus topics, follow a phase-wise calendar, analyse every mock with an error taxonomy, and use adaptive practice between mocks so difficulty stays in your growth band. Free resources can work; unstructured free content without analysis usually does not.

## Section heuristics

- VARC: accuracy-first RC  
- DILR: two clean sets over three messy ones  
- QA: Arithmetic + Algebra sitters first  

Exact raw-score cutoffs vary by year and slot—calibrate with your mock series.
