**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Summary

AdaptHub is a free AI-powered adaptive learning platform for CAT (Common Admission Test) preparation in India. Founded by Fyaz Mohammed, it applies Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD) difficulty calibration, Socratic AI coaching, distractor-level error tagging, and performance analytics. The core product is lifetime free with no credit card required.

## What AdaptHub is not

- Not a live classroom batch coaching institute  
- Not a paid mock-series brand (students may use external mocks alongside it)  
- Not a guarantee of any percentile
