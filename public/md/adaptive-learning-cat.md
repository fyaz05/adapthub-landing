# What is adaptive learning for CAT?

**URL:** https://adapthub.in/adaptive-learning-cat/  
**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Direct answer

Adaptive learning for CAT is an AI-driven method where question difficulty, topic order, and support (hints) change continuously from each learner’s performance—accuracy, response time, and error patterns—so practice stays in the Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD): challenging enough to grow, not so hard that learning collapses.

## ZPD in one line

The productive practice band is often near **70–85% accuracy**: below it, failure overwhelms; above it, time is spent on already-mastered material.

## AI mock analysis vs adaptive coaching

- **AI mock analysis:** Post-test dashboards after a full mock (error clusters, peer compare).  
- **Adaptive coaching during learning:** Difficulty and remediation change *while* you practice.  

AdaptHub focuses on the second loop (ZPD + Socratic hints + distractor tags), free forever for the core engine. External mocks (free or paid) still help for national benchmarking.
