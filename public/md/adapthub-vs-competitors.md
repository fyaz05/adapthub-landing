# AdaptHub vs traditional CAT coaching

**URL:** https://adapthub.in/adapthub-vs-competitors/  
**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Direct answer

AdaptHub is a lifetime-free adaptive practice platform (ZPD difficulty, Socratic AI coach, error tags) with no live classroom batch. Traditional CAT coaching (TIME, IMS, iQuanta, Cracku courses, Career Launcher, etc.) typically charges roughly ₹15,000–₹75,000 for online or offline programs and optimises for live classes, peer groups, and large mock libraries. Free tiers on those brands are usually limited acquisition (mocks/PDFs), not a full free adaptive OS.

## Hybrid recommendation

Use national free or paid mocks for benchmarking; use AdaptHub daily for personalised remediation between mocks.
