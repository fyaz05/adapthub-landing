# CAT DILR strategy 2026

**URL:** https://adapthub.in/cat-dilr-strategy/  
**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Direct answer

DILR is set selection under a 40-minute cap. Spend the first ~two minutes scanning all sets, pick two workable ones, and abandon traps early. Log selection mistakes separately from pure logic errors. Two high-accuracy sets usually beat three incomplete disasters.
