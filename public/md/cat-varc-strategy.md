# CAT VARC strategy 2026

**URL:** https://adapthub.in/cat-varc-strategy/  
**Updated:** 2026-07-13

## Direct answer

CAT VARC is won with RC accuracy first, not vocabulary cramming. Practise 2–3 passages most days across genres (philosophy, economy, science, culture), eliminate extreme options, and log inference vs detail mistakes. Add VA (para jumbles, summary, odd sentence out) as a daily micro-set; TITA VA often has no negative marking.
